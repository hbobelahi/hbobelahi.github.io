About Me

I am a visual artist and digital illustrator passionate about creating fantasy and science-fiction worlds. My work focuses on storytelling through image, character design and emotional atmosphere.

Alongside my visual practice, I am also the creator and writer of the original sci-fi fantasy project Eu-genica, where I combine illustration, narrative and world-building into one universe.
